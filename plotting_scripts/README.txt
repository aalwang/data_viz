The scripts in this directory produce plots including necessary statistics

List of scripts used for each plot in the paper "Dependence of Surface Fluxes and Clouds on Sea Ice Concentration Around Arctic Marginal Ice Zones in Observations and ERA5":

npole_map.py --> Figure 1
ts_multivar.py --> Figures 2,3, and 4
scatter_contour_plot.py --> Figures 5,6, and 7
binned_dep.py --> Figures 8,9, and 10
